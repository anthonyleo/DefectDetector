***************************************************************************
Start Up Process
***************************************************************************
1. Open Terminal
2. Copy and Paste '' and press enter
3. Enter your starting chainage number as your reference point
4. Camera feeds for left and right camera will open
5. Left click mouse into either of these camera feed windows
6. Travel along the rail to your inspection point (Hall Effect sensor will be incrementing distance as the wheel turns on the rail)
7. Pull inspection cameras below rail for inspection using the lever arms on the mechanism
8. Enter one of the following keyboard bindings depending on the situation:
      'a' - Left camera DEFECT, Right camera OK
      's' - Left camera DEFECT, Right camera DEFECT
      'w' - Left camera OK, Right camera DEFECT
      'd' - Left camera OK, Right camera OK

      Images will be saved of both camera feeds into the plugged in USB drive along with data saved into a txt file in the same USB drive.
9. Continue steps 6-8 until you reach the start of a new chainage point (reference point)
10. Once at your new chainage start position enter the following keyboard binding:
      'c' - The program terminal will ask you to enter a new chainage reference number
